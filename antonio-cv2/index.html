Who is he?

From an early age he begins his passion for music and especially for the saxophone. After finishing his classical degree, he begins to experiment with other musical styles like jazz, pop, electronic music... This will give him a wide musical range for his performances.
His talents as a musician and his desire to learn have led him to live in different countries of the world, this coexistence with other musicians and cultures have helped him to grow to a great level.
It can be said that he has experienced many musical aspects, from acting as a soloist in classical symphonic orchestra, big band, touring with pop band around the world, to get deep into electronic dance music.
All this experience has led him to perform in America, Europe, Africa and Asia, where he currently resides (Bangkok).
This musician will make the live performance a unique and wonderful experience, not to be missed.
